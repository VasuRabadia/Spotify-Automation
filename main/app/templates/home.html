TuneSort
Your music, your way.
AI-powered sorting by mood, genre, language, and your unique taste.

TuneSort takes your playlist name as input and uses Google Gemini AI to analyze and automatically sort your tracks.

By understanding the mood, genre, language, and your personal listening style, TuneSort reorganizes your songs into smart, curated playlists.

Whether you're looking to freshen up your library or create the perfect vibe, TuneSort makes playlist organization fast, intuitive, and personalized.
